FAMOUS MARKETS



Aabpara Market

One of the oldest and most famous markets in Islamabad and a popular shopping hub for Islamabadis, you can find clothes, shoes, consumer goods, kitchen essentials, dry fruits, sporting goods and a lot more at Aabpara Market. The market is located in Sector G-6, next to Khayaban-e-Suhrwardy.
 address:699 Khayaban-e-Suhrwardy, G-6/1 G 6/1 G-6, Islamabad, Islamabad Capital Territory 44000

Khan Market

  Khan Market, established in 1951, and named in honour of freedom fighter Khan Abdul Jabbar Khan, brother of Abdul Ghaffar Khan. It was ranked as the most expensive retail location in India in 2007. In 2019, it was rated as the world's 20th most expensive retail high street by real estate firm.
 Address:Street 50, G-10/3 G 10/3 G-10, Islamabad, Islamabad Capital Territory 44000

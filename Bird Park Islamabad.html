Bird Park Islamabad

See rare bird species in their natural habitat at Bird Aviary, an aviary housing over a thousand different species and providing birds with a safe, natural environment. See parrots, pheasants, doves, swans, ducks, peacocks, cranes, and many other species at the aviary. Go to the birdwatching tower to get a better look at the birds' environment and see more birds up close. The birds aren’t scared of people, so you can feed them, a treat that kids in particular enjoy.
